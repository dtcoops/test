Tile Drop

Overview:

In 2021 I took an online game design fundamentals course through VFS. One of the weekly assignments involved creating a level design document that demonstrated an intuitive introduction of game mechanics and a steady difficulty ramp. The sketch-up that I created had four levels, utilizing jumping mechanics with a double jump mechanic to traverse a maze of fallings tiles with patrolling enemies. I later found the design document that I made and I wanted to see the design implemented.

Goal:

  • Use level design to intuitively introduce mechanics with a steady difficulty ramp for levels and puzzles.
  • Implement fully functional levels with controllers, start and end states, UI, and sounds.
  • Explore the level design choices with blockout game demo.
