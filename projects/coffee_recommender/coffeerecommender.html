Coffee Recommender

Overview:

This is a coffee analysis project built for Simon Fraser University's CMPT 353 - Computational Data Science.

Goal:

I approached this project as an imaginary "Coffee Company" attempting to provide a new coffee product for coffee lovers. They want to select a new product, but want to ensure that it will be appeal to the demographic. To do so, I used data from James Hoffman's Great American Taste Test in combination with other coffee information datasets. This used these to find information about a specific subset of the american population (Hoffman's veiwers - and presumably coffee lovers) and attempted to discover patterns or preferences in their coffee drinking habits. I then wanted to use this information to align those preferences to specific coffee products to find what products would be the best to develop and offer.
